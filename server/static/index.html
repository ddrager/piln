piln

Piln is a prepaid IPFS pinning service, it will pin any object for any period of time, given the amount you pay. You can add credits to any object to extend the time it will stay pinned. A list of currently pinned objects is public and everyone can extend the pinning time of any object.

There are no registrations and payments are in Bitcoin over the Lightning Network.

The price is the cheapest in the market, at 77 satoshis per gigabyte per day, or 2310 satoshis per gigabyte per month.
