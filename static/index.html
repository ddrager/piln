piln

Piln was a prepaid IPFS pinning service you could use anonymously and instantaneously with the Bitcoin payments over the Lightning Network. It is closed for new pins since January 8, 2019. Objects that were pinned until that day are now stored on Eternum, a reliable IPFS pinning service.

The reason for the shutdown is that IPFS is a terrible technology and shouldn't be used or relied upon by anyone. If you want you can host your own Piln, the code is on GitHub, but I really don't recommend it.

Thank you very much for your time and support.
