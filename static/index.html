piln

Piln is a prepaid IPFS pinning service, it will pin any object for any period of time, given the amount you pay. You can add credits to any object to extend the time it will stay pinned. The list of currently pinned objects is public and everyone can extend the pinning time of any object. Nothing can be unpinned.

There are no registrations and payments are in Bitcoin over the Lightning Network.

The price is the cheapest in the market, at satoshis per gigabyte per day!

Since you can pay as much as you want and the pinned objects can have any arbitrary size, you have to do some calculation (calculation is done automatically if you have window.ipfs enabled in your browser). If you pay less than a day's worth of the selected object your request will be rejected.
